My Projects

APT reconstruction

Atom Probe Tomography (APT) is an advanced microscopy technique that employs field ion emission to extract individual atoms from a material, enabling a precise 3D reconstruction of its atomic structure. This project aims to enhance reconstruction accuracy by integrating crystal plane data.

Cadmium Zinc Telluride

This project focuses on understanding the defect structures in the CdZnTe alloy, a material widely used in medical imaging for X-ray detection. CdZnTe crystals often contain various structural defects, and gaining insight into these surrounding defects can directly contribute to improving the crystal growth process.
